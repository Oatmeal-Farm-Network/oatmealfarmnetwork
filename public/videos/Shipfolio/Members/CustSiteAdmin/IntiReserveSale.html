About Derwydd Peruvian Inti-Sapa
Inti continues to impress in the showring. At AlpacaMania 2007 he placed first (out of 6) in Get of Sire, and his fleece earned a blue ribbon in the Spin-Off (out of 4). On numerous occasions, Ian Watt, Editor and Publisher of the International Alpaca Handbook, has recommended Inti as a herdsire with outstanding fleece characteristics.

Inti is a winner, both in the show ring and in his bloodlines. He earned 8 blue ribbons between 2002-2004, including IOA, and he was Fawn Color Champion at the Monterey Pronk in 2003, and Reserve Color Champion at the Monterey Pronk 2004.

While Inti has many ribbons to show off, his progeny have won more: Inti had 4 Reserve Color Champion progeny at the 2007 Monterey Pronk: Reserve in Fawn Male (yearling), Reserve in Light Female (juvenile), Reserve in Light Male (2 yr old!). Fleece Reserve in Light (Age A) and Inti won 2nd Place in Get of Sire right behind Elite Maximus! Inti-Sapa's Sunrise won Fawn Color Champion in fleece at the 2006 AOBA Fiber to Fashion show and at the 2006 AlpacaFest West Fleece show!
